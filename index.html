Hi! I'm Jasper

I was born in 1990, and spent most of my youth in Lokeren & Ghent, Belgium. I currently live in Zürich and write code for Fugue Snyk.

Software

I love Haskell because it's a lot of fun to write, and you end up with robust and maintainable software. I think PureScript is a good alternative when you're targeting the browser. Here are some things I made:

  • 双曲番 (sōkyoku-ban): Hyperbolic Sokoban, playable in your browser
  • patat: Terminal-based presentations using Pandoc
  • Hakyll: A static website compiler library in Haskell
  • stylish-haskell: a highly configurable Haskell code prettifier
  • Beeraffe: a word mashup game
  • Lorem Markdownum: a lorem ipsum generator for markdown
  • A fair number of Haskell libraries and tools: websockets, psqueues, blaze-html, profiteur, ...

Community

  • Since 2017, I have organized ZuriHac, the largest Haskell community event in the world.
  • I organized the 2021 ICFP Programming Contest.
  • I was part of the Haskell.org committee for around 6 years. I was chair for half of that time and focused mostly on the Summer of Haskell program.
  • When I was at Ghent university, I was heavily involved in the Zeus WPI computer club.
